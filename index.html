О проекте

It’s become harder and harder for banks, credit unions, and other lending entities to be responsive to consumer trends and keep up with technology advances. While many financial institutions have embarked upon their digital transformation journeys, disjointed and uneven lending solutions have left them struggling to improve their efficiency and productivity and grow their businesses.

To address these problems our experts created Anovaa, a solution that answers the industry’s need for modernization, productivity, and efficiency across the lending lifecycle. Anovaa is the result of years of experience, invention, and unparalleled expertise. We’re bringing the future of lending to the present with our SaaS-based and turnkey white-label outsourcing solution that empowers lenders to master digital convergence and deliver better loans to help more people build their dreams.
